WELCOME TO OUR LIBRARY

Empowering the community through access, education, and imagination

By Colby Capraun

Our library is more than just shelves of books — it's a hub for learning, creativity, and connection. Whether you're here to discover a new novel, research a topic, or join a community event, we're here to support your journey.

We believe in open access to knowledge for all. That’s why we offer free resources, from books and audiobooks to public computers and workshops. Our staff is always happy to help you find what you need — or introduce you to something new.

Over the years, we’ve expanded our services to include online databases, digital lending, and educational programs for all ages. We’re committed to growing with our community and staying ahead of the curve in a changing world.

A library is not a luxury but one of the necessities of life.

Whether you're a student, a parent, a lifelong learner, or just someone looking for a quiet place to read — this space is yours. Come in, explore, and make yourself at home. We're glad you're here.

A Brief History

Of Our Library

  • Established - 1972

    Our library opened its doors with just 1,000 books and a single reading room. From the start, it was built on the belief that knowledge should be available to everyone.

  • 1990s Expansion

    We added a children's wing, public computers, and launched our first literacy programs for adults and families.

  • 2010 - Digital Leap

    We began offering eBooks, audiobooks, and access to digital learning tools. Wi-Fi and study spaces became key parts of our service.

  • Today

    With thousands of visitors each month and a collection that spans formats and generations, our library continues to grow and serve as a cornerstone of the community.
